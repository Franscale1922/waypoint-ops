## Article Structure

```
H1: [Final SEO Title]

[WIIFM lede — 2-3 sentences that speak directly to the candidate's situation]

## H2: [The Problem / What Most People Get Wrong]
[2–3 paragraphs]

## H2: [The Waypoint Framework / What to Actually Do]
[2–3 paragraphs with numbered or bulleted steps]

## H2: [What This Means FOR YOU]
[Candidate-specific application — what they do next]

## H2: Bottom Line
[2–3 sentences. No fluff. Clear next step.]
```

---

## Quality Checklist (before marking done)

- [ ] Primary keyword appears in: title, first paragraph, at least 1 H2
- [ ] AEO question is directly answered within the first 150 words
- [ ] Every H2 is written from the candidate's POV (not Waypoint's POV)
- [ ] Reads aloud like Kelsey — direct, no corporate fluff, no passive voice
- [ ] Has a clear single CTA at the end
